Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 16, 2025
Last Updated: May 16, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the Tracy's Karate Practice App (referred to as "the App", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of data:

a. User-Provided Data

  • Username (optional): Used to personalize your experience.
  • Practice Preferences: Delay settings, reminder times, and playlists.

b. Usage Data

  • Technique View History
  • Practice Sessions: Timestamps, techniques practiced, durations.
  • Flagged Techniques and Playlists
  • Achievements: Derived from app usage and stored for progress tracking.

c. Device Data

  • Anonymous User ID: Assigned via Firebase Authentication.
  • Push Notification Token: Used locally to send reminders (never shared).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Track progress and unlock achievements
  • Store preferences like delay, flags, and playlists
  • Send practice reminders (if enabled)
  • Improve app experience based on general usage patterns

We do not use your data for advertising or third-party analytics.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored in Firebase Firestore (Google Cloud), protected with encryption and strict read/write rules:

  • Only you (authenticated anonymously) can modify your data.
  • Public progress pages can be viewed by others if explicitly shared via link.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties. Sharing your stats is optional and done through manually generated links.

5. Your Choices

  • You can clear your data at any time from within the app.
  • Notification permissions can be managed through your device settings.
  • Uninstalling the app removes local storage; cloud data remains unless manually removed.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If this happens, contact us to have it removed.

7. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated. We will notify users of significant changes in the app. Continued use after updates implies acceptance.
